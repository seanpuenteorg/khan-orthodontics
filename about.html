About Us

Our Story

Khan Dental Liverpool was founded by Dr Khalid Khan on Allerton Road in the heart of South Liverpool's L18 — a practice that grew not through advertising, but through word of mouth from clients whose smiles spoke for themselves. Dr Khan earned his reputation as a celebrity dentist through meticulous cosmetic work on a client list that spans television personalities, musicians, and public figures many Liverpudlians will recognise from their screens. That reputation didn't happen by accident — it was built appointment by appointment, smile by smile, on a standard of cosmetic dentistry that refused to cut corners.

What sets Khan Dental apart from the dozens of dental practices across Liverpool is the direct access patients have to Dr Khan himself — a clinician whose cosmetic work has been scrutinised under the harshest of lights, literally. Every composite bonding case, every veneer, every smile makeover is approached with the same exacting eye that celebrity clientele demand. Patients on Allerton Road receive the same clinical standard as those whose smiles appear on billboards.

The practice operates as a fully private clinic, which means no NHS waiting lists, no rushed appointments, and no compromise on materials or technique. Whether you're coming in for a routine scale and polish, an emergency out-of-hours appointment, or a full cosmetic transformation, the experience at 121 Allerton Road is built around one outcome: a result you're genuinely proud of.
